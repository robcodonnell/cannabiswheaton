FUTURE PRODUCT POSITIONING

We enable our streaming partners to understand and reach the growing medical cannabis market by leveraging our brand building expertise to create brands and products that resonate with patients.

As Canada moves towards an adult recreational market, which will have strict marketing, advertising and labelling regulations, we can help our streaming partners prepare for and build their brands in order to grow customer loyalty.

DIVERSIFICATION

At Cannabis Wheaton we believe there is strength in numbers. We invest and support a wide range of cannabis cultivation companies that make up our roster of streaming partners each with a unique offering, based on their individual leadership teams and expertise, and specialized growing techniques and innovations.

Production is diversified across many streaming partners, reducing the risk associated with build-out delays, licensing delays, poor harvests, etc.  

Given the reduced production risk, Cannabis Wheaton represents the ideal partner for medical clinics and any other distribution channel.

UPSIDE

Currently, significant shareholder value is created as applicant licensed producers advance through the Health Canada licensing process; however, there is limited access and significant risk in investing in those still in the application stage of licensing.  

Cannabis Wheaton creates an opportunity for investors to access these licensed producers with significant upside potential, while mitigating risk through diversification, value and the expertise of our management team.

BEST BRANDS

Cannabis Wheaton provides our streaming partners with customized branding solutions that not only comply with the Canadian regulations, but that also speak to and resonate with the Canadian market. We also work with leading global cannabis brands that want to enter into the Canadian market.
