FUTURE PRODUCT POSITIONING

We enable our partners to capitalize on the growth of the medical cannabis market and the introduction of the recreational cannabis market by leveraging our brands building expertise to create brands and products that resonate with the consumer.

DIVERSIFICATION

Production is diversified across many streaming partners, reducing the risk associated with build-out delays, licensing delays, poor harvests, etc.

Given the reduced production risk, Cannabis Wheaton represents the ideal partner for medical clinics and any other distribution channel.

UPSIDE

Currently, significant shareholder value is created as licensed producers advance through the Health Canada licensing process; however, there is limited access and significant risk in investing in these licensed producers still in the application stage of licensing.

Cannabis Wheaton creates an opportunity for investors to access these licensed producers with significant upside potential, while mitigating risk through diversification, value and the expertise of our management team.
